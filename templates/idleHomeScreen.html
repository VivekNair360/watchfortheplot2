Origins

For a long time, the sports industry has dominated fantasy. But no longer. We introduce film forecast, a platform which allows all to fantasize about real plots and futures. Future up!

The idea for a competition platform similar to fantasy football but for the movie and TV show genre started in the HelloWorld 2018 Hackathon at Purdue University. We decided that there was a market of individuals just itching for the ability to compete among themselves for which of their theories were best. After all, when there is a record of your victory, it is that much sweeter to Watch for the Plot for next time.

What We're About

Submit futures, place priorities, make trades, and hope for your future!

Submit predictions in a format of competition of leagues. Even be an administrator of a league, where you decide the movie or TV show! From there, submit futures at your will, but always remember, the other members in your league will be reviewing your submissions for plausibility, so always think your futures out in detail! Then, we'll have you choose which futures you want from your favorite to your most hated, and we'll have a draft night to assign your predictions. Make your trades, submit more predictions, and motion for draft nights before the dead zone, because we will assign points based on whether your predictions were true!
